## Question Pattern Per Section

Every section follows the same 10-question structure:

| Position | Pattern | Single-select |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Q1 | Likert-Frequency | How often do you engage? |
| Q2 | Categories | What specifically matters? |
| Q3-Q4 | Preference/Logistics | How and where do you shop? |
| Q5 | Willingness to adapt | Import, travel, pay more? |
| Q6 | Importance rating | Key factor importance |
| Q7 | Services needed | What support services matter? |
| Q8 | Flexibility/trade-off | What would you compromise on? |
| Q9 | Brand vs. other factor | Priority weighting |
| Q10 | Section ranking | Force-rank the 5 key factors |

## Key Observation

Q100 is the **meta-ranking** — the user ranks all 10 sections against each other, giving Gemini the cross-section weighting it needs to prioritize Shopping & Services metrics.
